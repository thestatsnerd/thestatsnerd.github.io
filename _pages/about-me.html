Chris Morgan is a Data Scientist with The General where he combines his knowledge of statistics and business acumen to drive results. An innovative thinker who values continual personal development by way of intellectual curiosity, creativity, and diversity in ideas; Chris strives to be at the intersection of data and strategy, connecting business owners to actionable solutions by telling a better story. His current focus is in marketing analytics, customer behavioral analytics, and enhancing the digital experience.

From Minnesota originally, Chris completed his undergraduate studies in Mathematics at St. Olaf College in Northfield, MN, before earning an M.S. in Applied Statistics from Purdue University. Chris is a member of the advisory board for Austin Peay State University’s Professional Science Master’s program and is an active member of the American Statistical Association. He previously worked for Asurion, a global telecomm insurer based in Nashville, TN.

When not building predictive models to make customers’ lives easier, Chris enjoys playing Euchre, anything outdoors, traveling, and trying to solve humanity’s addiction to texting and driving. He resides with his wife, Amber, in Nashville.
